About

Pocket DJ is a small, portable, standalone DJ board built for people who want to mix anywhere. No laptop, no cables, no compromises. Load your tracks, press play, and start mixing.

Whether you're at a house party, a rooftop, or a park bench — Pocket DJ goes where you go. Everything you need is onboard: crossfader, EQ, cue points, and a built-in speaker. Designed to be as intuitive as it is portable.

Good gear is great, but good gear that fits in your bag is better.
